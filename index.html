What are Cheese Blintzes?

Cheese Blintz is a Jewish recipe that can be served for breakfast or as a dessert.

How to make Cheese Blintz:



Start by making the pancake batter in a medium bowl. Add the eggs and milk and beat them well.

Add the flour, sugar, vanilla extract and salt.

Mix it all together then place a piece of plastic over the bowl and let it chill in the fridge for an hour before making the pancakes.

While the batter is chilling, prepare the cheese filling by combining the ricotta cheese, cream cheese, egg, lemon juice and vanilla in a small mixing bowl.

Mix everything together until well combined. However, there may still be lumps left and that's okay, they will go away when cooked.

After the time is up, heat a small non-stick pan over medium heat. Then spray the pan with non-stick cooking spray.

Using a ladle, drop about 3 ounces of batter into the oiled pan and swirl it around so the batter spreads and becomes runny. It should cover the bottom of the pan.

Let the pancake cook for about 60 seconds, allowing it to fluff up and dry out.

Then flip the pancake over and let it cook for another 15 seconds on the other side. The blintz should be golden brown.

Transfer the pancake to a wire rack to cool when finished cooking.

Repeat these steps with each pancake until the batter is gone. Don't forget to spray the pan again with non-stick cooking spray.

After the pancakes have cooled, you will begin assembling the cheeseburgers.

Place a small amount of cheese filling on the bottom third of a pancake.

Then invert the bottom with the cheese filling halfway up the pancake. Then fold the two sides of the pancake in half.

Then fold the top down to finish. They will look like little cheese burritos.

Repeat these steps until all the pancakes are filled.

Finish the blintzes by browning them in the small pan with a little butter and brown both sides of the blintzes starting from the folded side down. This should take about 60 seconds.

Serve with your favorite toppings.

Recipe notes and tips:


Batting requires a step you don't normally take. Mix the dough ingredients and let it rest in the bowl for about an hour in the fridge. The flour expands and the texture of the batter improves. So be patient at that time.


To make up for this, you can prepare the blintzes in advance. Then all you have to do is stuff them and brown them just before serving.


I find that if you use a ladle you will get an even size. This makes rolling blintzes very useful. I like to prepare mine in either a small pan or a large baking pan if I need to make a few batches at once.


You can add powdered sugar, fruit, preserves, a small dollop of cream or even eat them plain. It does not matter.


How do you fold a blintz?


You will essentially be making little miniature burritos out of the little pancakes. It only takes 3 easy steps.


Place a small amount of the cheese filling on the bottom third of the pancake.

Turn the bottom part over with the cheese filling up to the middle of the pancake.

Fold the two sides of the pancake towards the middle.

How to Store and Reheat Cheese Blintze:


Once the blintzes are made, let them cool before storing. Then put them in an airtight container (preferably not a bag so they don't break) and refrigerate for about 5 days.


Then to reheat them put them on a baking sheet and bake them at 350 for about 5-10 minutes or until they are heated through. However, I think they are also delicious cold!


Can you freeze cheeseburgers?


These are good frozen! Place them in a container suitable for freezing and store them for up to 3 months. Just be sure to take them out and let them thaw before you're ready to bake them.
